Burger Recipe

Description

Burgers are very stacked sandwiches of pure flavor.

Ingredients

  • Burger Buns
  • Burger Patty
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Open the buns
  2. Put the cooked patty on the bottom bun
  3. Put lettuce and cheese on top of the patty
  4. Put the top bun on top of the cheese
